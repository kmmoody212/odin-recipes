Grape Jelly Meatballs

Made with just 4 ingredients and 5 minutes of prep time, these Grape Jelly Meatballs are a must for holiday parties and game days. With sweet grape jelly and zesty BBQ sauce, these crockpot meatballs are a tried and true addictive appetizer that everyone loves!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup grape jelly
  • 1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 32 ounces frozen meatballs

Steps:

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the barbecue sauce, grape jelly, and Worcestershire sauce; whisk until smooth and combined.
  2. Add in the meatballs and toss the meatballs until coated.
  3. Place the lid back on the crock pot and cook on low for 4 hours (or high for 2 hours), stirring occasionally toward the end
  4. Turn the slow cooker to warm and enjoy!
