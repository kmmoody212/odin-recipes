Beef Sliders

Some of the best sliders you'll have, that are incredibly easy to make! Great "kid" sized burgers or even better for "adult" sized snacks! NOTE: Feel free to use any kind of small rolls with this recipe. These sliders taste GREAT with Hawaiian rolls as well!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 8 ounces Velveeta cheese (cubed or use the shreds)
  • 0.5 (1 1/4 ounce) package onion soup mix
  • 1 sm onion, chopped fine
  • 2 (20 count) packages Pepperidge Farm rolls, in a pan (20 small in a package)
  • 40 slices dill pickles, about

Steps:

  1. Brown ground beef, and drain.
  2. Stir in cheese, soup, and onion until cheese melts.
  3. Cut buns in half.
  4. Spread burger mixture on buns.
  5. Wrap in foil.
  6. Bake 350 for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Open from foil and top each with pickle slice.
  8. Serve immediately.
